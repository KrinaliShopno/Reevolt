Why is regular health testing important?

Regular testing helps detect diseases at an early stage, ensures timely treatment, and prevents long-term complications.

How often should I get a full body health checkup?

Most adults should undergo a complete health checkup once a year, but those with chronic conditions (like diabetes, hypertension, or heart issues) may need more frequent testing.

Are prescribed medicines safe?

Yes, all medicines are prescribed only after accurate diagnostic results and are approved by qualified doctors following medical guidelines.

Can I book lab tests online?

Yes, you can easily book lab tests and doctor consultations online. Reports are delivered digitally and explained by our healthcare experts.
